Dr. Brainlove

Photo Courtesy of Shira Brettman

Dr. Brainlove is a climbable interactive brain mounted on a modified biofuel-powered school bus. Dr. Brainlove made its debut at Burning Man and will continue her life as a traveling neuroscience exhibit. Brains are vehicles for our thoughts, emotions, memories, and experiences, making Dr. Brainlove a quintessential vehicle for any mind-blowing adventure!
